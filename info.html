What to Expect

Meet the team. Hear about the research.

Come meet Access Insights — a group that includes former Intel innovators and researchers including Pete Denman (a prior QUAD resident and Board Chair) and Lex Frieden (an architect of the ADA). We'll introduce ourselves and tell you about the pilot study we are launching with QUAD.

Our goal is to give people with disabilities a voice in the design and development of products and services that will impact their lives. We hope this is the first step in an ongoing partnership with QUAD.

About the Study

The pilot study focuses on the experiences of people who live at QUAD — particularly the care and help request system. We want to learn what works well and what could work better.

At the gathering you may express your interest in this or any future studies. All residents are welcome whether or not they plan to participate in research.

Participation is fully voluntary. You will not be asked to share anything you consider confidential. All residents are welcome whether or not they plan to participate in research.
